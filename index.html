About Us

GoWWW is a non profit initiative that was started by Pranav Shikarpur (@snpranav) and Mohammed Suhail CS (@itsmesuhailcs). GoWWW connects early stage and amateur web developers with Non Profit Organizations, where you can contribute as a developer, receive Mentoring, and a lot of perks. This infrastructure under development will mutually benefit web developers by improving their portfolios (gaining experience with exposure) and Non Profit organizations by improving their reach over the web. Most importantly, there is mentorship and perks for selected web developers, so what are you waiting for?
